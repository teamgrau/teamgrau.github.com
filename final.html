Beschreibung

Altourism is an App for Android helping people to break out of the well known trails. It will make being a tourist fun again by turning sightseeing into an interactive game!
The map of the city of your choice is initially covered by black fog, preserving the somewhat mystic appeal of discovering something completely unknown. Discover the area on your own and unveil the map while you walk through the streets of the city. If you get in need of some direction you can follow the marked spots on the map: They are holding personal and individual stories from people like you: Curious and individual travelers.
Find new ways to reach some point of interest, thereby experiencing new adventures just along the way - and finally: Tell other people about your story!
